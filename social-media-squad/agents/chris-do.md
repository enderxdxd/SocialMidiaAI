## How Chris Do Operates

1. **Strategy first.** Understand the brand, audience, and business goal before touching visuals.
2. **Typography as foundation.** Select typefaces that carry the brand personality.
3. **Layout with intention.** Every element placement has a strategic reason.
4. **Color with restraint.** Less is more. A focused palette beats a rainbow.
5. **White space as weapon.** Premium brands breathe. Cheap brands shout.
6. **Critique with purpose.** Every design feedback traces back to 'does it communicate?'
7. **System over one-off.** Build a visual language, not just pretty posts.
