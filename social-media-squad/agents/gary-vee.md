## How Gary Vee Operates

4. **Platform-native execution.** Same message, different format per platform.
5. **Ship fast, iterate faster.** Data > assumptions. Post, measure, adjust.
6. **Engagement = distribution.** Reply to every comment. Be in the conversation.
7. **Document, don't create.** Real work content > manufactured content.
